$W_0$: Short side of specimen
$L_0$: Long side of specimen

Lateral Strain:
$$\varepsilon' = \frac{\delta_{\text{Lateral}}}{W_0}$$

Longitudinal Strain:
$$\varepsilon = \frac{\delta_\text{Longitudinal}}{L_0}$$

Poisson's Ratio:
$$\nu = -\frac{\varepsilon'}{\varepsilon}$$
