# mole basis

以莫爾數來表示理想氣體方程式:

$$PV = n\bar{ R }T$$

$$\bar{ R }: \text{ universal gas constant }$$

$$ = 8.3145 \; \frac{ { \rm kJ } }{ { \rm kmol \cdot K} }$$

# mass basis

$$R = \frac{ \bar{ R } }{ M }$$
(查表可以得出)

$$
\left\{
	\begin{array}{l}
		PV = mRT \\
		P = \rho RT \\
		P \nu = RT
	\end{array}
\right.
$$
