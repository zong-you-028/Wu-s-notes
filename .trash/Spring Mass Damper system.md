這個系統會自然產生[[常微分方程]]

![[Pasted image 20210801155550.png]]

$m$: mass
$k$: spring constant, $f_s = kx$
$c$: damping coefficient, (assume viscous damping)
$$\Sigma F = -kx-c{\dot {x}}+f = ma = m\ddot{x}$$
$$\implies m\ddot{x} + c\dot{x} + kx = f$$


> viscous: 有黏滯性的
> $\dot{x} = \frac{dx}{dt}$
> $\ddot{x} = \frac{d^2x}{dt^2}$
