# 太空系統組成

一個標準的太空系統會包含這些部門

- Space System
	- Launch Segment
		- Launch Facility
		- Launch Veihicle
		- Launch Control
	- Space Segment
		- Ground Handling Equipment
		- Spacecraft Bus + Payload
		- Payload Support Equipment
	- Ground Segment
		- Flight Operations
		- Communication Services
	- Data Segment
		- Data Processing Facility

與 [[太空任務]] 的分法有點相似
